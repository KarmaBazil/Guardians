The Guardians of the Galaxy Franchise is part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The first film was released in 2014 and the second film was released in 2017. The Guardians are also featured in Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame, and Thor: Love and Thunder, released in 2018, 2019, 2022.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 will be released into cinemas next year for the Summer blockbuster season, more specifically on Friday, May 5, 2023. This will make the third Guardians installment the second film in the MCU's busy Phase 5 schedule; the halfway point of the Multiverse Saga.
